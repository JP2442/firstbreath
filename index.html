Our Mission Statement

Our Mission is to be able to offer a resource page for Pediatric Cancer patients to learn about their conditions, and what is being done to help them through their journeys. Down below you'll find links and such to organizations, foundations and etc, who have truly dedicataed themselves to trying to find a cure, and making the lives of those affected happier.

What is Leukemia and Lymphoma?

The National Pediatric Cancer Foundation(NPCF) is a organization that is especially dedicated to funding the research through their fundraisers. Want to help move their research forward?

The American Childhood Cancer Organization is a non-profit organization made specfically for children with cancer. Like the other charities above, they are trying to work to help find solutions for young cancer patients.

The Children's Cancer Unit Charity is a charity based in Ireland. In this unit, children affected by their conditions are cared for by the experienced mediacal staff.
